Global Design. Design Leadership.

Global Design Leadership Association is a non-profit association advancing the convergence of design and leadership  to create social value and expand global collaboration and knowledge ecosystems.

CLAB, its research laboratory, manifests this mission as human-centered innovation  — designing the future of human value through research, education, and enduring practice.
